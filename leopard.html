Fun Facts About Leopards

← Back to Main Page

Leopard Fact #1

Leopards are known for their incredible strength and ability to climb trees even while carrying heavy prey.

Leopard Fact #2

Leopards can run at speeds of up to 58 kilometers per hour.

Leopard Fact #3

They are solitary animals and usually only interact during mating or raising cubs.

Leopard Fact #4

Leopards are incredibly adaptable and can live in a variety of habitats, from deserts to rainforests.

Leopard Fact #5

Unlike other big cats, leopards are excellent swimmers and enjoy being in the water.
