Fun Facts About Lions

← Back to Main Page

Lion Fact #1

Lions are the only cats that live in groups called prides!

Lion Fact #2

Lions can roar so loudly that it can be heard up to 8 kilometers away.

Lion Fact #3

A lion's mane is a sign of its age and health. The darker the mane, the healthier the lion.

Lion Fact #4

Lions spend about 20 hours a day resting and conserving energy.

Lion Fact #5

Male lions defend their pride's territory, while females do most of the hunting.
