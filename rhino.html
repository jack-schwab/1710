Rhino Fact #1

Rhinos are some of the largest land mammals, weighing up to 2,300 kg!

Rhino Fact #2

Their horns are made of keratin, the same material as human nails and hair.

Rhino Fact #3

Some rhino species can run at speeds of up to 55 km/h!
